Welcome to the Theremin Simulator

Experience the unique sound of a theremin right in your browser!

Click or touch to control pitch and volume without physical contact.

How to Play the Theremin

1

Start Playing

Click or touch the theremin area to start producing sound.

2

Control Pitch

Move your cursor or finger up and down to change the pitch.

Higher position = higher pitch

3

Control Volume

Move your cursor or finger left and right to change the volume.

Right position = louder

4

Customize Sound

Use the control panel to change waveform and add effects.
